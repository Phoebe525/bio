🚀 Introduction to the Nervous System

The human nervous system is a fascinating and complex network that acts like the body's supercomputer! It sends messages at lightning speed, helping you move, feel, and think.

🧠 The nervous system has two main parts:

  • Central Nervous System (CNS): The brain and spinal cord, the control center.
  • Peripheral Nervous System (PNS): A vast network of nerves that connect the CNS to the rest of your body.

🌟 The Peripheral Nervous System is further divided into two key parts:

  • Sympathetic Nervous System: This is your body's "fight or flight" system! When you're in danger or under stress, it increases your heart rate, dilates your pupils, and boosts your energy.
  • Parasympathetic Nervous System: Known as the "rest and digest" system, it helps slow your heart rate, relax your muscles, and promote digestion after a stressful event.
