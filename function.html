⚡ The Functions of the Nervous System

The nervous system is like a high-speed communication network that keeps your body running smoothly! Here are its key functions:

  • 👀 Sensory Input: Detects stimuli from the environment (e.g., light, sound, touch).
  • 🧠 Integration: The brain processes sensory input and decides on the appropriate response.
  • 💪 Motor Output: Sends signals to muscles and glands to execute responses like movement or secretion.
  • ⚖️ Homeostasis: Maintains balance in the body, regulating temperature, blood pressure, and other vital functions.

🚨 Factors Affecting the Nervous System

  • 🧪 Toxins & Drugs: Substances like alcohol, nicotine, and drugs can disrupt nerve function.
  • 🍎 Nutrition: Deficiency in essential vitamins (e.g., B12) can harm nerve health.
  • 💤 Sleep: Lack of sleep affects brain function, memory, and mood.
  • 💪 Physical Activity: Exercise enhances neural connections and brain health.
  • 😨 Stress: Chronic stress can damage nerves and impair cognitive function.

🩺 Common Nervous System Disorders

  • 🧠 Alzheimer's Disease: A degenerative brain disorder causing memory loss and confusion.
  • ⚡ Epilepsy: A neurological disorder leading to recurrent seizures.
  • 🦠 Multiple Sclerosis (MS): An autoimmune disease that damages nerve coatings, disrupting communication.
  • 🦵 Parkinson’s Disease: A disorder affecting movement due to low dopamine levels.
  • 🛏️ Stroke: A sudden loss of brain function due to interrupted blood supply.
